Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 09, 2023

Key Points

We value your privacy and want to be clear about how we handle your information:

Data Collection

We collect essential information needed for submissions.decino.nl to operate, including submission details and contact information.

Data Usage

Your information helps us process submissions, communicate with you, and maintain service security.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data and can exercise your privacy rights at any time.

Information We Collect

Submission Information
  • WAD files and download URLs
  • Level names and specifications
  • Author information (if provided)
  • Email addresses for communication
  • Technical details about submissions
Technical Data
  • Browser type and version
  • Access times and dates
  • Pages visited on our website
  • Technical information necessary for service delivery

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses
  • Processing and managing your submissions
  • Communicating about submission status
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Maintaining service security and preventing abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

Data Sharing and Security

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights and property
  • With your explicit consent
Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. However, no electronic transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Privacy Rights

Your Rights Include
  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data (where applicable)
  • Objection to data processing
  • Data portability

Retention Period

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
